Lesson 1: Scanning



What will you learn from this lesson?

This lesson will take you through the following topics with the help of text based learning, images, videos and interactive 3D simulations:

1) Components of the ATOS Scanbox 6130  2) Basics of pre-scan 3) The scanning procedure

By the end of this lesson, you should be familiar with how an object, large or small is scanned on the ATOS Scanbox 6130 along with an overview of all the basic procedures involved.

Evaluation Pattern:

There will be an evaluation test at the end of this lesson to gauge how much you have learnt. The test will be consist of a series of 10 questions in MCQ format with each question having only one correct answer. The passing criteria for the test is 80% after which the further modules will open up. You can re-take the test a maximum of 3 times.
