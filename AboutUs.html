About Us

Advancing deployment of highly sophisticated robotic technologies to sustain economic and productivity growth seems to be the order for the future. Considering that leveraging robotic technologies is as much as understanding the specific domain knowledge that is being built, the SMART platform aims to develop a scaffold for creating the ontological taxonomy of the subject matter.

SMART proposes a structured knowledge management framework to efficiently capture, create, and transfer knowledge to users. The platform systematically scaffolds and embodies the declarative, procedural and causal knowledge of the expertise. In other words, the ontological management system emulates the cognitive structure of knowledge building – what, where, when, how and why - that is much in-line with how novices query and nurture heuristic knowledge.

SMART facilitates learning to upskill the workforce to operate highly specialized robotic technologies. In fact, SMART provides experiential coaching to advance operators’ capability to manipulate and navigate the robots as well as helping novices accumulate experiences to perfect their expertise.
