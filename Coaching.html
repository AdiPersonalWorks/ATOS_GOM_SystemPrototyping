Welcome to the SMART Learning Hub !

 

What will you learn here?

Through the SMART Learning hub you will be able to:
1) Learn the latest advanced manufacturing technologies
2) Gain in-depth exposure into working of industrial machinery
3) Have a hands-on experience in virtual reality

Every module provides a comprehensive step by step approach which will allow you to learn freely at your own pace. Interactive videos and 3D models have been included to make the learning experience richer.

Evaluation Pattern

There will be a mandatory evaluation test at the end of every module to gauge how much you have learnt. The test will consist of a series of 10 questions in MCQ format with each question having only one correct answer. The passing criteria for the test is 80% after which the further modules will open up. You can re-take the test a maximum of 3 times.
