QUERY SEARCH



1. What is the basic process of calibration?

The calibration is done by using two rectangular plates with tesselations. The camera mounted on the robot arm moves through all its degrees of freedom and calibrates accordingly.

2. What is the basic process of scanning?

Once the part is made scan ready it is placed on a refernced position on the platform. From here one of the things is possible: 1.If the part has already been scanned before then the scanning routine of the previous scan will be adopted. 2.If no scanning has been done previously then the camera movement positions for each scan path will have to be set manually using the software to form the entire scanning routine. This process is tedious and can take anywhere between one and a few hours.
